APAH SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM

At APAH, education is our top priority. We believe that young people are the future. Since APAH began in 2009, we’ve awarded over $13,000 in scholarships to Houston residents pursuing a higher education with a degree in architecture or architecture related majors such as urban planning and interior architecture.
